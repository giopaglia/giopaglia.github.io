--- title: Why AI? icon: fa-search order: 5 ---

I've always enjoyed automating things, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the essence of automating hard tasks. A hard task is one that you as a human can carry out, but have trouble in teaching a digital computer to, perhaps because there is no “perfect” procedure for it, or because the thought process required for solving it is complex and unclear, even to you. Machine Learning, today's primary enabler of AI, seeks the solution in data; in fact, data has the answers to many hard questions, and oftentimes you just have to word your questions in the right way.
